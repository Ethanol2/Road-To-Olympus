They have 5 days to travel the distance.

Progress Tracker:
Everytime the player moves the screen goes dark and it tells them in km how much further to olympus

Random Encounters:
Some encounters will happen on the path (Enemies, travelers, gods in disguise)
Others will happen off trail, the player will choose to divert

Villages:
Players can buy food, sell goods, or buy a bed for rest

Gods:
Players can earn favours from gods, in the form of instant rest, food, money and stat buffs. If they piss them off, they can have negative effects
